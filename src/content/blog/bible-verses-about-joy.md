## Joy in God's presence

> *"Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is fulness of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore."* — [Psalm 16:11 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%2016%3A11&version=KJV)

> *"This is the day which the LORD hath made; we will rejoice and be glad in it."* — [Psalm 118:24 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%20118%3A24&version=KJV)

> *"I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the LORD."* — [Psalm 122:1 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%20122%3A1&version=KJV)

> *"Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all ye lands. Serve the LORD with gladness: come before his presence with singing."* — [Psalm 100:1–2 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%20100%3A1-2&version=KJV)

> *"Then will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy."* — [Psalm 43:4 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%2043%3A4&version=KJV)

## Joy from Jesus

> *"These things have I spoken unto you, that my joy might remain in you, and that your joy might be full."* — [John 15:11 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=John%2015%3A11&version=KJV)

> *"Ye now therefore have sorrow: but I will see you again, and your heart shall rejoice, and your joy no man taketh from you."* — [John 16:22 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=John%2016%3A22&version=KJV)

> *"Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith; who for the joy that was set before him endured the cross, despising the shame, and is set down at the right hand of the throne of God."* — [Hebrews 12:2 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Hebrews%2012%3A2&version=KJV)

> *"Whom having not seen, ye love; in whom, though now ye see him not, yet believing, ye rejoice with joy unspeakable and full of glory."* — [1 Peter 1:8 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=1%20Peter%201%3A8&version=KJV)

> *"Blessed are ye, when men shall revile you, and persecute you, and shall say all manner of evil against you falsely, for my sake. Rejoice, and be exceeding glad: for great is your reward in heaven."* — [Matthew 5:11–12 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Matthew%205%3A11-12&version=KJV)

## Joy in suffering

> *"My brethren, count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations; Knowing this, that the trying of your faith worketh patience."* — [James 1:2–3 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=James%201%3A2-3&version=KJV)

> *"For his anger endureth but a moment; in his favour is life: weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning."* — [Psalm 30:5 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%2030%3A5&version=KJV)

> *"They that sow in tears shall reap in joy."* — [Psalm 126:5 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Psalm%20126%3A5&version=KJV)

> *"Yet I will rejoice in the LORD, I will joy in the God of my salvation."* — [Habakkuk 3:18 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Habakkuk%203%3A18&version=KJV)

> *"As sorrowful, yet alway rejoicing; as poor, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things."* — [2 Corinthians 6:10 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=2%20Corinthians%206%3A10&version=KJV)

> *"Now the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, through the power of the Holy Ghost."* — [Romans 15:13 KJV](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Romans%2015%3A13&version=KJV)

## A short word

Biblical joy is fed by God's nearness, not by the weather of circumstance. The same source — His presence, His Spirit, His promises — feeds the joy of the festal pilgrim and the joy of the prisoner.
